How to Play: Each round, players are dealt a hand of cards (starting with 1 card, going up, then back down). Before play, everyone bids how many tricks they think they'll win. Score 10 + bid if exact, or 1 per trick if not. Trumps beat all other suits. No helping the dealer make the total add up!
